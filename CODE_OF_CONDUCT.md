# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

Our pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, trolling, or abusive comments
- Discrimination based on race, gender, or other protected characteristics

Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will remain confidential.

For full details, see the Contributor Covenant at https://www.contributor-covenant.org
